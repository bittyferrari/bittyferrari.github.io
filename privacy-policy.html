Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how this application ("Bluetooth MultiLayout Keyboard") handles user information.

The App is a Bluetooth keyboard application that allows users to use their Android device as a wireless keyboard for other devices. The App supports keyboard input features including Chinese Zhuyin (Bopomofo) input.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

We do not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location information
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media files
  • Account information
  • Payment information
  • Usage analytics data

Bluetooth Permission

The App uses Bluetooth functionality to communicate with compatible devices.

Bluetooth is used only for providing keyboard input functionality. The App does not collect or send Bluetooth-related information to any external server.

Keyboard Input Data

Keyboard input data is processed locally on your device and transmitted only through the Bluetooth connection to the paired target device.

The App does not store, record, or upload your keyboard input data.

File and Storage Access

If the App provides optional file or storage-related features, access is used only to provide the requested functionality.

Files are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server.

Data Sharing

The App does not sell, share, or transfer user information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services that collect user information.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user privacy. Since the App does not collect or store personal information, there is no personal data stored on our servers.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
