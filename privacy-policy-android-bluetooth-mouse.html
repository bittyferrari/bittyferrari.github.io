Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Bluetooth Mouse application ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. The App does not gather your name, email address, location, contacts, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

2. Bluetooth Usage

The App uses your device's Bluetooth solely to connect to and control another device as a mouse/pointer. All Bluetooth pairing and communication happens locally between your devices. No data from these connections is recorded, stored, or sent to us or any third party.

3. Permissions

The App may request Bluetooth-related permissions (such as Bluetooth Connect and, where required by the operating system, Nearby Devices or Location) only to discover and connect to Bluetooth devices. These permissions are used exclusively for the App's core functionality and never for tracking or data collection.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for users of all ages.

6. Data Security

Because the App does not collect or store any personal data, there is no user data at risk. All operations run locally on your device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
